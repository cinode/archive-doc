Static Splitted File Blob
#########################

This blob is used to hold the content of
files of size of at least 16MB.

The content of this blob starts with an integer
holding the cumulative size of the file. It's then
followed by an array of entries,  every entry holding
two strings - first one being an id of the blob holding
the content or blob's part, the secone beong an encryption
key fo the blob.
